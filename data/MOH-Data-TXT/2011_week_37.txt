Influenza Situation in Singapore 2011
Influenza indicators for E-week 37 (11 - 17 September 2011) are as follows:
The number of patients seeking treatment in polyclinics for Acute Respiratory Infections (ARI) was 14,299 in E-week 37 (daily average of 2600 over 5.5 working days), compared to 13,175 in E-week 36 (daily average of 2395 over 5.5 working days).

    The proportion of cases with Influenza-Like Illness (ILI) among the polyclinic ARI cases was low at 1%.
