Influenza Situation in Singapore 2011
Influenza indicators for E-week 38 (18 - 24 September 2011) are as follows:
The number of patients seeking treatment in polyclinics for Acute Respiratory Infections (ARI) was 14,452 in E-week 38 (daily average of 2,628 over 5.5 working days), compared to 14,299 in E-week 37 (daily average of 2,600 over 5.5 working days).
