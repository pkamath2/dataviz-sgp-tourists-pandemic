Influenza Situation in Singapore 2011
Influenza indicators for E-week 33 (14 - 20 August 2011) are as follows:
The number of patients seeking treatment in polyclinics for Acute Respiratory Infections (ARI) was 14,387 in E-week 33 (daily average of 2,616 over 5.5 working days), compared to 11,788 in E-week 32 (daily average of 2,620 over 4.5 working days).
