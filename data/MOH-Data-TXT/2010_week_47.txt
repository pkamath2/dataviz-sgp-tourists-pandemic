Influenza Situation in Singapore 2010
Influenza indicators for E-week 47 (21 - 27 Nov 2010) are as follows:
The number of patients seeking treatment in polyclinics for Acute Respiratory Infections (ARI) was 13,363 in E-week 47 (daily average of 2,430 over 5.5 working days), compared to 10,936 in E-week 46 (daily average of 2,430 over 4.5 working days).
