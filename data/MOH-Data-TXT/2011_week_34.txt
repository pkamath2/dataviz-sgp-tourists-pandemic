Influenza Situation in Singapore 2011
Influenza indicators for E-week 34 (21 - 27 August 2011) are as follows:
The number of patients seeking treatment in polyclinics for Acute Respiratory Infections (ARI) was 12,491 in E-week 34 (daily average of 2,498 over 5 working days), compared to 14,387 in E-week 33 (daily average of 2,616 over 5.5 working days).

    The proportion of cases with Influenza-Like Illness (ILI) among the polyclinic ARI cases was low at 1%.

Of all the influenza virus isolates collected in August 2011, influenza A (H3N2) constituted 70%, whilst influenza A (H1N1-2009) and influenza B constituted 10% and 20% respectively .
