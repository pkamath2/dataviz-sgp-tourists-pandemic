Influenza Situation in Singapore 2011
Influenza indicators for E-week 31 (31 July - 6 August 2011) are as follows:
The number of patients seeking treatment in polyclinics for Acute Respiratory Infections (ARI) was 14,146 in E-week 31 (daily average of 2,572 over 5.5 working days), compared to 14,278 in E-week 30 (daily average of 2,596 over 5.5 working days).
