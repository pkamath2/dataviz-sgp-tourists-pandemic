Influenza Situation in Singapore 2011
Influenza indicators for E-week 11 (13 - 19 Mar 2011) are as follows:
The number of patients seeking treatment in polyclinics for Acute Respiratory Infections (ARI) was 13,763 in E-week 11 (daily average of 2,502 over 5.5 working days), compared to 15,743 in E-week 10 (daily average of 2,862 over 5.5 working days).
