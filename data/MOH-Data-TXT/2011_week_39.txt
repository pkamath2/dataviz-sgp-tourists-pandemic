Influenza Situation in Singapore 2011
Influenza indicators for E-week 39 (25 September - 1 October 2011) are as follows:
The number of patients seeking treatment in polyclinics for Acute Respiratory Infections (ARI) was 14,611 in E-week 39 (daily average of 2,657 over 5.5 working days), compared to 14,664 in E-week 38 (daily average of 2,666 over 5.5 working days).
