Influenza Situation in Singapore 2011
Influenza indicators for E-week 44 (30 October - 5 November 2011) are as follows:
The number of patients seeking treatment in polyclinics for Acute Respiratory Infections (ARI) were 15,007 (daily average of 2,729 over 5.5 working days) in E-week 44, compared to 12,827 (daily average of 2,850 over 4.5 working days) in E- week 43.
