                                                                   Influenza Situation in Singapore 2010
Influenza indicators for E-week 41 (10 Ð 16 Oct 2010) are as follows:
The number of patients seeking treatment in polyclinics for Acute Respiratory Infections (ARI) was 13,413 in E-week 41 (daily average of 2,439 over 5.5 working days), compared to 13,564 in E-week 40 (daily average of 2,466 over 5.5 working days).
