Influenza Situation in Singapore 2011
Influenza indicators for E-week 45 (6 - 12 November 2011) are as follows:
The number of patients seeking treatment in polyclinics for Acute Respiratory Infections (ARI) were 13,218 (daily average of 2,937 over 4.5 working days) in E-week 45, compared to 15,007 (daily average of 2,729 over 5.5 working days) in E- week 44.
