Influenza Situation in Singapore 2011
Influenza indicators for E-week 42 (16 - 22 October 2011) are as follows:
The number of patients seeking treatment in polyclinics for Acute Respiratory Infections (ARI) were 15,585 (daily average of 2834) in E-week 42, similar to to 15,862 (daily average of 2884 over 5.5 working days) in E-week 41.
