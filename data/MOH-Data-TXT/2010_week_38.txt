Polyclinic Attendances for Acute Respiratory Infection
Overall, influenza activity in Singapore in E-week 38 was low.
2009 2010 Epidemic Threshold Warning level
Week
The proportion of cases with Influenza-Like Illness (ILI) among the polyclinic ARI cases remained low at 2%.
Among patients with ILI in the community, 25% had influenza B, 8% had influenza A (H1N1-2009) and 10% had influenza A (H3N2) (preliminary).
