 Influenza Situation in Singapore 2010
Influenza indicators for E-week 51 (19 - 25 Dec 2010) are as follows:
The number of patients seeking treatment in polyclinics for Acute Respiratory Infections (ARI) was 11,156 in E-week 51 (daily average of 2,231 over 5 working days), compared to 13,484 in E-week 50 (daily average of 2,452 over 5.5 working days).
