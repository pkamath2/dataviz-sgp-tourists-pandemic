                                                                         Influenza Situation in Singapore 2010
Influenza indicators for E-week 46 (14 - 20 Nov 2010) are as follows:
The number of patients seeking treatment in polyclinics for Acute Respiratory Infections (ARI) was 10,936 in E-week 46 (daily average of 2,430 over 4.5 working days), compared to 13,528 in E-week 45 (daily average of 2,460 over 5.5 working days).
