 Influenza Situation in Singapore 2010
Influenza indicators for E-week 50 (12 - 18 Dec 2010) are as follows:
The number of patients seeking treatment in polyclinics for Acute Respiratory Infections (ARI) was 13,484 in E-week 50 (daily average of 2,452 over 5.5 working days), compared to 13,962 in E-week 49 (daily average of 2,539 over 5.5 working days).
