Influenza Situation in Singapore 2010
Influenza indicators for E-week 49 (5 - 11 Dec 2010) are as follows:
The number of patients seeking treatment in polyclinics for Acute Respiratory Infections (ARI) was 13,962 in E-week 49 (daily average of 2,539 over 5.5 working days), compared to 13,308 in E-week 48 (daily average of 2,420 over 5.5 working days).
