Influenza Situation in Singapore 2011
Influenza indicators for E-week 50 (11 December - 17 December, 2011) are as follows:
The number of patients seeking treatment in polyclinics for Acute Respiratory Infections (ARI) were 13,298 (daily average of 2,418 over 5.5 working days) in E-week 50, compared to 13,964 (daily average of 2,539 over 5.5 working days) in E- week 49.

    The proportion of cases with Influenza-Like Illness (ILI) among the polyclinic ARI cases was low at 1%.
