                                                               Influenza Situation in Singapore 2010
Influenza indicators for E-week 40 (3 Ð 9 Oct 2010) are as follows:
The number of patients seeking treatment in polyclinics for Acute Respiratory Infections (ARI) was 13,564 in E-week 40 (daily average of 2,466 over 5.5 working days), compared to 13,950 in E-week 38 (daily average of 2,536 over 5.5 working days).
