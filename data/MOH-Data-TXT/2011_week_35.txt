Influenza Situation in Singapore 2011
Influenza indicators for E-week 35 (28 August - 3 September 2011) are as follows:
The number of patients seeking treatment in polyclinics for Acute Respiratory Infections (ARI) was 11,124 in E-week 35 (daily average of 2,472 over 4.5 working days), compared to 12,491 in E-week 34 (daily average of 2,498 over 5 working days).

    The proportion of cases with Influenza-Like Illness (ILI) among the polyclinic ARI cases was low at 1%.
