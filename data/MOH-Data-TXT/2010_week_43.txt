Influenza Situation in Singapore 2010
Influenza indicators for E-week 43 (24 -30 Oct 2010) are as follows:
The number of patients seeking treatment in polyclinics for Acute Respiratory Infections (ARI) was 13,795 in E-week 43 (daily average of 2,508 over 5.5 working days), compared to 13,212 in E-week 42 (daily average of 2,402 over 5.5 working days).
