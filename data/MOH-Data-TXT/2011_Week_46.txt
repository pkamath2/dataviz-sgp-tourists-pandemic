Influenza Situation in Singapore 2011
Influenza indicators for E-week 46 (13 - 19 November 2011) are as follows:
The number of patients seeking treatment in polyclinics for Acute Respiratory Infections (ARI) were 14,692 (daily average of 2,671 over 5.5 working days) in E-week 46, compared to 12,409 (daily average of 2,758 over 4.5 working days) in E- week 45.
